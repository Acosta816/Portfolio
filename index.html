"Animal Quiz"

2019

Description

A Short and simple quiz application that tests your knowledge of random, strange, and little-known animal facts. The app boasts a cartoon jungle background with a score keeper and “current question” indicator. The user is presented with 4 multiple choice questions and a related image. After answering, each question is provided feedback with the correct answer. At the end, depending on the user’s score, a final score screen is shown with a specific “emblem” to match the user’s ranking as well as a Try Again button.

Created using: *HTML5, *CSS, *jQuery, *Javascript
